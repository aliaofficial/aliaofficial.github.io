Alia is a Lua executor created for game developers. The purpose of the tool is to give game developers who create anti-cheat a way to test freely without constantly renewing keys.

Staging Process

These outline the process of your application submission, payment information and more.

Form Submission

To apply for this tool, you must meet these requirements:

  • Developer or/and owner of a game, with evidence provided
  • The game must have an average of around 10+ players daily, with evidence provided
  • You must have at least an average of 5,000 robux spent in the game monthly
  • Provide evidence of a working, legitimate anti-cheat system built into your game
  • Able to pay $1.99 USD weekly or every two weeks
  • No bad reputation or background of exploiting, hacking or malicious activity

Wait 1-2 Weeks

Within 1-2 weeks, if you meet the application requirements, you will be accepted. You will then receive an email explaining how to install or inject Alia into Roblox on iOS, along with a key that renews every two weeks depending on your payment.

You can cancel anytime after each payment has been created. Once canceled, your key will no longer be valid and you are free to delete the application.

Remember this

  • All keys are hardware-ID issued. Redistribution of keys or the app is not possible.
  • Decrypting Alia as an .ipa and redistributing it, or modifying its files to allow any key, is not allowed and is considered piracy.
